Design Patterns & Color Theory

Color Theory

The design utilizes a rich dark violet-maroon gradient theme (`#20040F` to `#3B1423`) combined with premium glowing rose-gold accents. This creates a velvet, premium corporate appearance, communicating high-end services and creative excellence.

Information Design Patterns

- **Hierarchy:** Services list (Logo Designing, Business Cards, Website, UI/UX, Research) are cleanly organized with clear line icons and high-contrast typography.
- **Modern Grid:** A balanced dual-column grid divides the page into visual showcase branding (left) and technical info checklist (right), giving readers a clean, structured visual path.
