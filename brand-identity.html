The Design Philosophy

Every logo must serve as a functional brand asset. It needs to remain legible at microscopic scale (e.g. favicons) and stand out at giant sizes (e.g. billboards). We prioritize geometric balance, custom grid layouts, and memorable silhouettes.

Design Patterns & Color Theory

Color Psychology

- **Pink Wave Nails:** Uses high-contrast deep maroon/crimson combined with clean whites to represent luxury and professional personal care.
- **IT General:** Combines dynamic gold and deep navy blue representing reliability, growth, and tech authority.
- **Urvi Loop Nest:** Soft pastel pink and dark charcoal fonts representing hand-made friendliness, comfort, and loop art.

Geometric Design Patterns

All logos are mapped using circular grid guidelines and golden ratio proportions. Typography is set in modern, geometric sans-serif (e.g. Outfit and custom modified vectors) to ensure modern appeal and high legibility.
