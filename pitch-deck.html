Design Patterns & Color Theory

Color Theory

The aesthetic uses a soft, natural cream/beige palette (`#F4EFEB` / `#E8D8CD`) which creates a cozy, literary, and welcoming mood. Headings and focus highlights use deep mulberry/brown tones (`#5C2E3B`) to command authority without the harshness of pitch black.

Design Patterns & Visual Hierarchy

- **Illustration Integration:** Integrating digital painting elements directly into layout slides, blending technical text blocks with warm illustrations.
- **Grid Structure:** Employs an asymmetrical editorial layout with spacious white margins, keeping slides readable, clean, and highly engaging for corporate presentations.
