Design Patterns & Color Theory

Color Theory

The background uses a luxurious, deep burgundy/velvet red tone (`#4A0A15`) representing premium quality, exclusivity, and passion. Contrast is achieved using warm, metallic gold vectors (`#D4AF37`) for branding emblems, and clean, high-contrast white details (`#FFFFFF`) for contact and operational info.

Typography & Layout Patterns

- **Typography:** Custom serif font system for headings to convey class, prestige, and historical authority, paired with a neat, geometric sans-serif for clear readability of phone numbers, address, and name text.
- **Composition Pattern:** Dual-sided layout. Side A focuses purely on logo branding and line-art illustration, creating visual balance. Side B presents clean vertical hierarchies for professional corporate details.
